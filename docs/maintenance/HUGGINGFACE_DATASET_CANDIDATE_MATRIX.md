## Scoring Legend

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **License** | High | 10 = permissive, 0 = unknown/restrictive |
| **Domain** | High | 10 = directly relevant, 0 = off-topic |
| **Quality** | High | 10 = high quality, 0 = garbage |
| **Alignment** | Medium | 10 = Bazzite-aligned, 0 = contradictory |
| **Rewrite** | Medium | 10 = easy to rewrite, 0 = impossible |
| **Security** | High | 10 = no risk, 0 = high risk |
| **Privacy** | High | 10 = no risk, 0 = high risk |

**Total Score:** Sum of all factors (max 70)  
**80%+ of max (56+):** Strong candidate  
**60–79% of max (42–55):** Moderate candidate  
**< 60% of max (< 42):** Reject
